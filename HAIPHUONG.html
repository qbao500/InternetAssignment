    Hai Phuong - the latest film starring and producing by Ngo Thanh Van, was officially released to Vietnamese audiences on February 22. The film is the journey of a mother named Hai Phuong to find her daughter Mai kidnapped.

    From a genre perspective, the film fulfills quite well the mission of an action / adventure / sensational film with a story that is not too unpredictable, the events taking place in a rush, high-dose fight scenes and information. Simple but impressive message. Out of the cinema, most viewers will be more impressed with the movie's visuals than the content.
