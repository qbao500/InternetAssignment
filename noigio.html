    The first film of the "Vietnamese Revolutionary Film" to talk about Vietnam War in the context of Southern of Vietnam against the US.

    During the American invasion of Vietnam, in Phuong's family, there was Van, who followed the Vietcong of South Vietnam, and Phuong, who was the lieutenant of the Saigon army. After many years of separation, the two siblings met again. When Van learned that Phuong was a lieutenant of Saigon army, she chased Phuong away.

    Van and her son were arrested in a concentration camp. In the camp Van participated in the struggle. Her son was killed by the enemy, and Van went crazy. After being released from prison, Van convinced her younger brother and many Saigon soldiers to switched side and killed American adviser..
