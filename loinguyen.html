    Three Medical University students who stumble upon a wormwood made with human blood in an ancient wooden box containing a piece of withered tree and amulet in the messy bookcase in Professor Hoan Sinh's house. That is wormwood, an esoteric medicine of the tribe Sang La.

    The three students look for wormwood to a large, dark house where Chieu Duong - a beautiful but pale, 16-year-old girl, is worshiped as a Saint for miraculously curing others. They discovered in the back garden Chieu Duong has a strange tree that looks like a giant blood vessel with thorns that like to suck human blood then cut a branch to take to the lab. From here, they are haunted by evil hallucinations and gradually discover the story of the bloodthirsty worm, its healing power as well as its unrivaled magical power. Wormwood blood is no longer a legend. Poor victims wish they had not made this discover, unfortunately, it was too late ...
