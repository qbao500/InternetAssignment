    The film's content is adapted from anecdotes about Vietnamese characters of the same name in Vietnamese folklore. The story of the film revolves around Quynh - a smart, clever man who loves to make fun of people in the village.

    Quynh secretly loves to remember her Diem - the daughter of the teacher Doan. He just tried so hard to flirt with people in his dream but he didn't succeed. Song, talented girl integrity is soon caught the eye of Trinh Ba. He conspired to harm Teacher Doan to force her marry him. Refusing to give in, Diem, Quynh and his best friend Xam wrapped up a prayer card for reporting. However, the journey to claim justice is really arduous because all authority in the land belongs to Lord.
